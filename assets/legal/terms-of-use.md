## 2. What EQ Cards is

A digital wallet for the Australian construction and trade workforce. You add your work licences, certificates and induction records; we render them in a tap-to-copy form so you can fill site induction forms quickly.

We are not a substitute for the original licence document. The wallet is a **convenience layer** — the underlying licence and its issuing authority remain authoritative. If a site requires the physical card, you must still present it.
