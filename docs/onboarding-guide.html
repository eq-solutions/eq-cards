EQ Cards — member guide

Getting started with your wallet

Keep your licences and tickets in one place — ready to show on site.

Step 1 — Getting in
Invited by your employer
  1. Your manager sends you a link by SMS or a QR code to scan.
  2. Open the link — you'll see your name and who sent the invite.
  3. Tap Sign in, verify your mobile, then tap Activate my account.
  4. Your wallet opens — any details your employer loaded are already there.
If you got a QR or join code (not a personal link), enter the mobile your manager used to register you, then tap Find my invite.
Signing up yourself
  1. Open the app or go to cards.eq.solutions.
  2. Tap the Mobile tab and enter your Australian mobile number.
  3. Tap Send code — a 6-digit SMS arrives within a minute.
  4. Enter the code. You're in.
Have a company code from your employer (e.g. sks)? Tap Join with a join code on the sign-in screen instead.
Step 2 — Adding a licence

Scan Fastest

Tap + in the top-right of your Wallet, then choose Scan a licence. Point your camera at your licence card. The app reads your number, name, and expiry. Review the result, then tap Save.

Enter manually

Tap +, choose Enter a licence manually, and fill in the type, number, and expiry date yourself.

Step 3 — Adding a certificate

Scan Fastest

Go to the Certs tab and tap + (or use + from the Wallet tab). Choose Scan a certificate. Works for First Aid, White Card, Working at Heights, and other site tickets. The app reads the expiry date — check it, then save.

Enter manually

Choose Enter manually and type in the certificate type, issuer, and expiry.

Step 4 — Showing a licence on site

QR verification Supervisor-friendly

Open a licence from your wallet and tap the QR code icon in the top-right corner. Your supervisor scans it and sees your verified licence details instantly — no app needed on their end. You can also copy the link and send it as a text message.
